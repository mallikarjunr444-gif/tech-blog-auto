Instead of: "The battery is good"

- Camera: Add specific locations tested, rival comparisons
- Performance: Add actual FPS numbers, temperature readings
- Battery: Show full-day drain timeline

#### STEP 3: Reduce AI Language
Search for and eliminate:
- ❌ "proof of the phone's excellent"
- ❌ "it's a great option for"
- ❌ "without any issues"
- ❌ "seamlessly"
- ❌ "cutting-edge"
- Replace with specific examples instead

#### STEP 4: Add Comparative Analysis
For each section, ask: "How does this compare to [Galaxy S23/OnePlus/Pixel]?"
Answer with specific differences, not vague claims

#### STEP 5: Create "Who Should Buy" Section
Segment audience into:
- 🟢 Perfect for: [3-4 specific user types]

- 🔴 Not ideal for: [2-3 user types]

#### STEP 6: Strengthen Verdict
Include:
- Clear rating (e.g., 4.2/5.0)
- Specific recommendation (which variant/version)
- Honest pros & cons with explanations
- Actionable buying guidance
