## 💡 PRO TIPS

### Time-Saving Tips:
- **Batch research:** Research 3-5 phones at once, write later
- **Template reuse:** Minor variations save time on later articles
- **Spreadsheet tracking:** Keep all specs in one place for easy updates
- **Calendar reminders:** Set monthly price update reminders

### Quality-Improving Tips:
- **Write, wait, edit:** Don't edit immediately after writing
- **Read aloud:** Catches awkward phrasing
- **Get feedback:** Have someone else fact-check
- **Link analysis:** Check what top competitors link to
- **Keyword research:** Use free tools (Google Trends, AnswerThePublic)

### SEO Tips:
- **Internal linking:** Link between your articles
- **Update regularly:** Add "Updated: [date]" to signal freshness
- **Long-tail keywords:** Target "best phone under ₹70,000" not "best phone"
- **Topical clusters:** Group related articles together
- **External links:** Cite authority sources for backlink potential
