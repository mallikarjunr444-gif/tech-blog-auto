**Purpose:** Tactile, real-world feel and durability

**Must Include:**

#### SECTION 3: DISPLAY & BRIGHTNESS (180-220 words)
**Purpose:** Real-world screen usability, not just specs

**Must Test:**
- Size and how it feels in hand (comparison helpful)
- Color accuracy (videos, photos, text rendering)
- **Brightness in Indian sunlight** (critical for Bengaluru/Delhi/Mumbai users)
- Refresh rate: when do you actually notice it? (not just 120Hz claim)
- Contrast and black levels
- Viewing angles (IPS vs AMOLED behavior)

**Tone Example:**

This is crucial because Bengaluru's sun is brutal. The 120Hz refresh rate is beautiful when scrolling Instagram, but honestly, I barely noticed it while watching YouTube videos or reading news. The real win is the peak brightness—something no spec sheet tells you.

**Purpose:** Gaming, apps, multitasking in real conditions

- Heating: location and when it happens
- Battery drain during gaming

**Real Example:**

The back of the phone, especially above the battery, reached about 38°C (hot but not dangerously so). My hands got uncomfortable after 90 minutes, not from performance lag, but from the heat. If you play BGMI daily, you'll love the first 45 minutes, then notice the thermal throttling.
